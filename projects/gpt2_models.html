Playing with GPT2 Language Models

Origin

I wanted to originally train a chat bot on random twitter accounts like the followers of Donald Trump or Thoughts of Dog, but the issue I had with this is that the tweets were such a small dataset (and the tweets of DT followers were incomprehensible) that the chat bot just wasn't able to produce great answers. Eventually Cards Against Humanity did a holiday promotion where they trained the GPT-2 model on their cards. I learned that the GPT-2 model was trained on 40GB and was available to the public. It understood english grammer and could be retrained to fit whatever I wanted it to.

Design

I had to fix some file paths and such put eventually I got it to start training. I thought about what I wanted to train this model on to see how good it was. I realized that if it could be trained on fanfic and write and capture the style of those fanfics then that would show how powerful this was. So I scraped a 10MB of Sonic fanfiction from the internet (this was from 1 author, all of Shakespeares works is around 5.5MB). I trained it and attempted to use my GPU but found out the model wouldn't fit in the memory and trained it on my CPU instead. It was trained for about a day and enough and provided samples that I honestly though were written by an edgy preteen. Now that I knew the model was good I trained it on other things, from Avatar the Last Airbender scripts to the headlines of various satire sites like the Onion. This entertained me so much because how realistic the results were, but still silly enough to be make one almost question if it was an original or a generated sample.
